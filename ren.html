Scythe of the Syndicate

Ren is a dragon originally known as the Boundarywyrm Kyurem. They were discovered within the space between worlds and were captured by the Syndicate.

While in containment, the organization planned to use Kyurem for a set of experiments known as the Synthetic Shapeshifting Project. They wanted to give a human access to all of the Boundarywyrm's power in order to turn the dragon into their very own superweapon.

The result is the dragon-human fusion that walks Grastaea today. Unwilling to fulfill the Syndicate's sinister plans, Ren broke out of containment. In the present day, they work alongside the Prince and the rest of the Halidom to protect everyone from harm.

Dragon, human, or...?

Because of their strange origins, Ren is surrounded by many rumors.

Some believe that Kyurem is an otherworldly fiend whose violent arrival wiped out an entire village. It is said that this "human-eating monster" lurks the ruins at night and attacks those who dare approach it.

Others believe that Ren is a human possessed by an evil spirit, similar to Morsayati and his reincarnations.

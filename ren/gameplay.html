Twinkling Flurry
Shareable 5
SP Cost: 3380
Shared SP Cost: 8450

Deals damage to enemies directly ahead (4 hits of 124%, 2 hits of 284%, and 1 hit of 396% water damage) inflicts frostbite (dealing 41% water damage every 2.9 seconds for 21 seconds - with 120% chance), and restores HP to the user with 30% Recovery Potency. If the user has Resonant Memory, a variant called Subzero Slash will be used instead.

Subzero Slash deals damage to enemies directly ahead (1 hit of 750% water damage and 1 hit of 834% water damage) and raises the dragon gauge by 50 DP if the attack connects. Afflicted foes take extra damage (1 hit of 975% water damage and 1 hit of 1084.2% water damage).

If this skill is used as a Helper Skill or a Shared Skill, it will deal 4 hits of 124%, 2 hits of 284%, and 1 hit of 396% water damage to enemies directly ahead, inflict frostbite for 21 seconds - dealing 41% water damage every 2.9 seconds - with 120% chance, and restore HP to the user with 30% Recovery Potency. (Might: 300)

Frozen Surge
SP Cost: 9063

Deals damage to surrounding enemies (3 hits of 480% water damage), reduces their defense (15% for 10 seconds), and grants all teammates the "Overdrive" effect. The defense reduction does not stack.

The skill gauge for this skill can be filled by attacking enemies, but it will also gradually fill automatically.

Overdrive has three progressively more powerful levels.
Lv. 1: For 60 seconds, increases strength by 10%.
Lv. 2: For 60 seconds, increases strength by 15%.
Lv. 3: For 60 seconds, grants overdamage based on 20% of the user's strength, and increases attack rate by 10%.

If Overdrive is granted while already active, it will be raised one level. Conversely, it will be lowered one level when the effect's remaining time is depleted. If the effect is at level one when this time is depleted, it will instead be removed entirely.

If the user has Resonant Memory, a more powerful variant called Fusion Strike will be used instead.

Fusion Strike deals damage to enemies in a line (3 hits of 768% water damage), reduces their defense (20% for 10 seconds) and grants all teammates the “Overdrive” effect. Afflicted foes take extra damage (3 hits of 1152% water damage). The defense reduction does not stack. (Might: 200)

Abilities
Boundarywyrm's Echo II

Reduces susceptibility to freezing by 100%.

Fills 50% of the dragon gauge at the start of quests and extends shapeshift time by 100%, but the maximum amount of the dragon gauge that abilities can fill at the start of quests will be limited to 50% for the entire team and doubles the amount of dragon energy required to shapeshift (The user's maximum dragon energy will not be increased).

When shapeshifting, the user will transform into Kyurem regardless of what dragon they are equipped with. While they are shapeshifted, this ability grants them a dragon strike that deals damage to enemies directly ahead and enemies near those targets.

Additionally, inflicts the “Forgotten Memory” debuff on the user at the start of quests. During Forgotten Memory, the user cannot shapeshift. Also, grants the user a charge gauge. The charge gauge fills when standard attacks and force strikes successfully connect. Filling the bar will consume “Forgotten Memory” and grant the user Resonant Memory. The user's charge gauge will not fill when they have the Resonant Memory effect. (Might: 100)

Resonant Memory grants the user the following effects:
  • Summons Boundarywyrm's Shadow. While summoned, Boundarywyrm's Shadow will attack together with the user.
  • The user's standard attacks are changed.
  • The user's force strikes are changed. They will also inflict frostbite and dispel one enemy buff.

Upon shapeshift, Resonant Memory will be consumed. When their shapeshift is undone, the charge gauge will be reset and Forgotten Memory will be reapplied. (Might: 100)

Skills
Twinkling Flurry
Shareable 5
SP Cost: 3380
Shared SP Cost: 8450

Deals damage to enemies directly ahead (4 hits of 124%, 2 hits of 284%, and 1 hit of 396% water damage) inflicts frostbite (dealing 41% water damage every 2.9 seconds for 21 seconds - with 120% chance), and restores HP to the user with 30% Recovery Potency. If the skill is used during dragondrive, a variant called Subzero Slash will be used instead.

Subzero Slash deals damage to enemies directly ahead (1 hit of 750% water damage and 1 hit of 834% water damage) and fills the user's dragondrive gauge by 450 UTP. Afflicted foes take extra damage (1 hit of 975% water damage and 1 hit of 1084.2% water damage).

If this skill is used as a Helper Skill or a Shared Skill, it will deal 4 hits of 124%, 2 hits of 284%, and 1 hit of 396% water damage to enemies directly ahead, inflict frostbite for 21 seconds - dealing 41% water damage every 2.9 seconds - with 120% chance, and restore HP to the user with 30% Recovery Potency. (Might: 300)

Frozen Surge
SP Cost: 9063

Deals damage to surrounding enemies (3 hits of 480% water damage), reduces their defense (15% for 10 seconds), and grants all teammates the "Overdrive" effect. The defense reduction does not stack.

The skill gauge for this skill can be filled by attacking enemies, but it will also gradually fill automatically.

Overdrive has three progressively more powerful levels.
Lv. 1: For 60 seconds, increases strength by 10%.
Lv. 2: For 60 seconds, increases strength by 15%.
Lv. 3: For 60 seconds, grants overdamage based on 20% of the user's strength, and increases attack rate by 10%.

If Overdrive is granted while already active, it will be raised one level. Conversely, it will be lowered one level when the effect's remaining time is depleted. If the effect is at level one when this time is depleted, it will instead be removed entirely.

If this skill is used during dragondrive, a more powerful variant called Fusion Strike will be used instead.

Fusion Strike deals damage to enemies in a line (3 hits of 768% water damage), reduces their defense (20% for 10 seconds) and grants all teammates the "Overdrive" effect. Afflicted foes take extra damage (3 hits of 1152% water damage). The defense reduction does not stack. (Might: 200)

Abilities
Boundarywyrm's Memory II

Reduces susceptibility to freezing by 100%.

Also, grants the user a dragondrive gauge and changes the shapeshift button into a dragondrive button. Tapping this button activates their dragondrive.

Dragondrive grants the following effects:

  • The user's standard attacks are changed and fill the user's dragondrive gauge if they connect.
  • The user's force strikes are changed and deal damage to enemies directly ahead. They will also fill their dragondrive gauge by 300 UTP if they connect, inflict frostbite and dispel one enemy buff. (Might: 100)

Kinda Like Him! II

Reduces susceptibility to burning and stun by 100%. (Might: 100)

Aura Boost II

While the "Overdrive Lv. 3" effect is active, the following effects are granted:

  • The user's strength is increased by 15%.
  • The user's skill gauge fill rate is increased by 10%.
  • All elementally attuned damage taken by the user is reduced by 15%. (Might: 100)

Notes

The "Overdrive" effect is immune to Curse of Nihility.

Boundarywyrm's Memory grants Ren access to a Dragondrive gauge, which allows them to use a Unique Transformation instead of Shapeshifting. This uses Unique Transformation Points (UTP), a variant of Dragon Points (DP).
